Every purchase you make isn't just a transaction—it's a step towards making a positive impact.

Your Contribution to Education & Community Development

When you choose Zera, you're directly contributing to a brighter future for Thailand. A portion of our profits is allocated monthly to support several key initiatives

Mercy Christian School

By supporting this charitable school, your purchase helps provide scholarships that focus on teaching ethics and morality, ensuring that the next generation is grounded in values that matter.

Learn More

Charisa Technological College

Education is a powerful tool for change. Through our donations to Charisa Technological College, we ensure students not only gain knowledge but also job security, equipping them for a successful future.

Learn More

Mercy Foundation

Our contributions to the Mercy Foundation go towards community development projects, ensuring that the beauty and integrity of Thai communities are maintained and strengthened.

Strengthening Communities Through Events

Our commitment goes beyond financial aid. NATHAN is deeply involved in organizing and participating in events that help strengthen relationships and improve lives within Thai communities.

Post-Tsunami Reconstruction

In the wake of the devastating 2004 tsunami, we played a significant role in rebuilding houses, helping to restore normalcy and hope for affected families.

Sports Competitions

We believe in the power of sports to unite communities. Our sports events across different districts foster camaraderie and healthy competition.

Festive Celebrations

Our Christmas events are a highlight, filled with shows, games, giveaways, and joyous festivities, bringing smiles to faces and warmth to hearts.
