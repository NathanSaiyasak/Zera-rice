Mission

Zera’s mission is to offer the highest quality Thai rice to the world, while nurturing the land and lives of our local farming communities. We are dedicated to sustainable practices, cultural authenticity, and social responsibility. Every Zera product not only feeds bodies but also fuels the prosperity of Thai heritage and the empowerment of our growers.

OUR PRODUCT

Premium
Jasmine Rice

Zera takes pride in presenting our premium Jasmine rice, a true gem harvested from the fertile plains of Northeastern Thailand. Known for its delicate floral aroma and subtle pandan-like fragrance, our Jasmine rice is a long-grain variety that is cherished globally for its distinctive taste and fluffy, slightly sticky texture after cooking. Cultivated with care by experienced farmers, Zera's Jasmine rice boasts a pearly white appearance, signifying its purity and quality.

  • Delicate Floral Aroma
  • Slightly Sticky
  • Fluffy Texture
  • Distinctive Taste
  • Pandan-like Fragrance
  • Long-Grain Variety
